NAME
    gcloud observability - manage Observability resources

SYNOPSIS
    gcloud observability GROUP [GCLOUD_WIDE_FLAG ...]

DESCRIPTION
    Manage Observability resources.

GCLOUD WIDE FLAGS
    These flags are available to all commands: --help.

    Run $ gcloud help for details.

GROUPS
    GROUP is one of the following:

     scopes
        Manage Scope resources.

     trace-scopes
        Manage Trace Scope resources.

NOTES
    These variants are also available:

        $ gcloud alpha observability

        $ gcloud beta observability
