NAME
    gcloud service-health - request events that are relevant to your project or
        organization

SYNOPSIS
    gcloud service-health GROUP [GCLOUD_WIDE_FLAG ...]

DESCRIPTION
    Request events that are relevant to your project or organization.

GCLOUD WIDE FLAGS
    These flags are available to all commands: --help.

    Run $ gcloud help for details.

GROUPS
    GROUP is one of the following:

     events
        Represents events that may affect Google Cloud products.

     organization-events
        Represents events that may affect products used across the
        organization.

     organization-impacts
        Represents impact to assets at organizational level.

NOTES
    These variants are also available:

        $ gcloud alpha service-health

        $ gcloud beta service-health
