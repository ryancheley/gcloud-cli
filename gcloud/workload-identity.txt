NAME
    gcloud workload-identity - manage Workload Identity

SYNOPSIS
    gcloud workload-identity GROUP [GCLOUD_WIDE_FLAG ...]

DESCRIPTION
    Manage Workload Identity.

GCLOUD WIDE FLAGS
    These flags are available to all commands: --help.

    Run $ gcloud help for details.

GROUPS
    GROUP is one of the following:

     service-agents
        Manage Service Agents for Workload Identity.

NOTES
    These variants are also available:

        $ gcloud alpha workload-identity

        $ gcloud beta workload-identity
